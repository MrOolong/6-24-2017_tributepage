Sade (Singer)

Sade Adu

Sade is the lead singer of the critically acclaimed soul/smooth jazz band of the same name. She was born Helen Folasade Adu in Nigeria, to a Nigerian father and a British mother. She started her singing career with a North London based Latin funk band called Pride. This led to her discovery and eventual signing with the Epic record label. Her 1984 single "Your Love Is King" helped launch her career after it reached the top 10 on the British hit list.
